Support Groups

If you or someone you know needs help, consider reaching out to these support organizations:

  • Gamblers Anonymous - A community-based support group for people with gambling disorders.
  • National Council on Problem Gambling (NCPG) - Resources for individuals and families affected by problem gambling.
  • Responsible Gambling Council - Resources and support to promote responsible gambling practices.

Counseling Services

For personalized counseling support, please reach out to licensed professionals through the following services:

  • BetterHelp - Online counseling platform offering licensed therapists.
  • Talkspace - A mobile platform that connects you to licensed therapists for remote counseling.
